Tadka Dahi is a popular Indian raita recipe that is made by tempering yogurt with spices and herbs. Here's a simple recipe to make Tadka Dahi at home:

An easy and simple flavoured yoghurt-based curry recipe made by with creamy curd with garlic and spice based seasoning. it is an ideal curry recipe which can be easily served with any choice of flatbreads or even as raita with rice-based dishes. the recipe can be made with different types of curd with different types of consistency, but the ideal one is thick and cream sour yoghurt for this recipe.

Dahi Tadka Recipe

An Indian Recipe for Masala Yogurt that is Dahi tadka.

Prep Time: 2 mins Cook Time: 5 mins Total Time: 7 -10 mins Servings: 1-2

Ingredients:

  • 2 cup curd
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp ghee / clarified butter
  • 10 clove garlic, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • ½ tsp mustard
  • pinch hing
  • 2 dried red chilli
  • few curry leaves
  • ¼ tsp cumin powder
  • ½ tsp coriander powder
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 2 tbsp coriander, finely chopped

Instructions:

  1. Firstly, in a large bowl take 2 cup curd and ½ tsp salt.
  2. Whisk and mix well making sure the curd turns smooth texture. keep aside.
  3. In a pan heat 2 tbsp ghee and saute 10 clove garlic until it turns golden brown.
  4. Now add 1 tsp cumin, ½ tsp mustard, pinch hing, 2 dried red chilli and few curry leaves.
  5. Splutter the tempering making sure to keep the flame on medium.
  6. Turn off the flame and add ¼ tsp cumin powder, ½ tsp coriander powder, ¼ tsp turmeric and 1 tsp chilli powder.
  7. Saute until the spices turn aromatic.
  8. Pour the tempering over curd and mix well.
  9. Also add 2 tbsp coriander and mix well-combining everything well.
  10. Finally, enjoy dahi tadka with roti or paratha.
