DAL KHICHDI



By Krishna Thakkar

Last Updated: 15 oct,2023

This Khichdi recipe is a light and easy to digest Indian dish made with rice and moong dal. Nutritious and light on the tummy, serve this healthy, comforting and nourishing porridge-like Dal Khichdi with curd, raita or papad.The simplest khichdi recipe version is made with just Ghee (clarified butter), cumin seeds (optional), and turmeric, which is prescribed in Ayurveda for detoxifying the body. A few herbs or spices like ginger, black pepper, asafoetida, can be added depending on the dosha type.

The kind of spices, herbs, veggies you add make a great difference to the flavors. In fact in Indian cuisine, there are so many ways we make khichri. I add onions and tomatoes to my moong dal khichdi – this is my usual, go-to basic recipe for regular, at-home cooking.



STEPS TO MAKE


1.Firstly, Rinse ½ cup moong lentils and ½ cup rice together. Then soak both of them for about 30 minutes in water. When the lentils and rice have had time to absorb water, drain all of the water and set aside.

2. Saute: Now heat 2 tablespoons ghee or oil in a 3 litre pressure cooker, then add 1 teaspoon cumin seeds (jeera).

3. As soon as the cumin begins to splutter, add the ⅓ cup finely chopped onions.

4. Sauté until the onions soften and turn translucent. Note: You do not need to sauté the onions until they are light or golden brown.

5.When the onions have become translucent, add in ⅓ to ½ cup chopped tomatoes, ½ teaspoon chopped green chili and 1 teaspoon chopped ginger.

6.Mix well with the sautéed onions.

7. After stirring in the tomatoes, add ¼ teaspoon turmeric powder and a pinch of asafoetida (hing). Adding hing is optional. Tip: For a gluten free khichdi, skip the asafoetida or use a gluten-free asafoetida.

8. Now Sauté until the tomatoes soften stirring often. Next, add the drained rice and moong lentils to the pressure cooker, and stir for about a minute or until all the ingredients are fully combined.Add some salt to taste.

10.Mix and sauté the rice and lentils for a minute.

11. Add 3.5 cups water and mix really well, deglazing the bottom and sides of the cooker. 3.5 cups of water will give you a porridge like consistency. For a thinner consistency, add 4 to 4.5 cups water.

12. Finally, close the lid tightly and pressure cook for 8 to 9 minutes or 7 to 8 whistles on medium-high to high heat. When the pressure drops on its own in the cooker (which takes some minutes), then only open the lid.Stir the khichdi.

13. Serve your delicious Moong Dal Khichdi hot or warm! You can also top it with a bit of ghee before serving.

Dal Khichdi Easy Recipe

Khichdi recipe is a delicious one pot meal of rice and moong lentils. Moong Dal Khichdi is light and easy to digest healthy food.

Prep Time: 30 mins Cook Time: 15 mins Total Time: 45 mins Servings: 2-3

Ingredients:

  • 0.75 cup moong dal,0.75 cup rice,water
  • 1.5 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 3 tablespoons Ghee
  • 0.5 cup finely chopped onions or 1 small to medium-sized
  • 0.5 cup chopped tomatoes or 1 medium-sized
  • 0.75 teaspoon chopped green chillies
  • 1.5 teaspoon finely chopped ginger
  • 0.38 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 5.25 cups water
  • salt as required

Instructions:

  1. Rinse and wash moong lentils and rice together. Soak both rice and lentils for 30 minutes in water.After 30 minutes, drain all the water and set aside.
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a 3 litre pressure cooker. Add cumin seeds. When cumin splutters then add onions.
  3. Sauté onions until translucent. No need to make onions light brown or golden brown.
  4. Once the onions become translucent, then add tomatoes, green chilli and ginger.
  5. Mix and add turmeric powder and asafoetida. Mix again and sauté until the tomatoes softens.
  6. Add the rice and moong lentils to the pressure cooker.
  7. Stir and sauté for a minute. Add water and season with salt.
  8. Mix well. Check the taste of the water and it should be slightly salty.
  9. Close the lid tightly and pressure cook on a high heat for 7 to 8 whistles or 8 to 9 minutes on medium-high to high heat.
  10. When there is no pressure in the cooker, remove the lid and check the consistency. If it looks too thick, add some hot water and mix well.
  11. Serve moong dal khichdi hot or warm with curd (yogurt) and accompanying salad.
